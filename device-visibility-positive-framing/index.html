Empowering Wildfire Prevention Through Accessible Information

Smokey Open Data is your trusted resource for comprehensive, up-to-date wildfire prevention and environmental stewardship data. Our platform brings together datasets from federal, state, and local agencies, making it easier for researchers, policymakers, and the public to access and utilize critical information for protecting our forests and communities.

By providing open access to wildfire statistics, prevention strategies, and educational resources, Smokey Open Data supports informed decision-making and community engagement. Whether you’re a student, scientist, or concerned citizen, our goal is to foster collaboration and innovation in wildfire prevention—because only you can help prevent wildfires.
